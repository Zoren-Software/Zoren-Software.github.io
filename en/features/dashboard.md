# Dashboard

The VolleyTrack Dashboard provides a comprehensive and centralized view of all important metrics and information in your system. Track the performance of players, teams, and trainings in one place.

## üìä Overview

![Dashboard](./images/dashboard.png)

The Dashboard is the first screen you see when logging into the system. It presents consolidated information about players, teams, trainings, and attendance analyses.

## üëã Welcome Section

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find:

- **Welcome Message**: "Welcome to VolleyTrack!"
- **Subtitle**: "Start managing your volleyball teams in minutes."
- **Initial Configuration**: Link to "View Initial Configuration" that helps you set up the system for the first time.

---

## üë§ Individual Analysis (TOP 3)

This section displays the three players with the best performance in the system.

### Information Displayed

For each player, you'll see:

- **Avatar**: Colored circle with the player's initial
- **Name**: Player's full name
- **Position**: Player's position on the team (e.g., Setter, Outside Hitter, Opposite)
- **Attendance Percentage**: Percentage of attendance in trainings
- **Training Statistics**: Format "X/Y/Z Trainings" where:
  - X: Number of presences
  - Y: Total number of trainings
  - Z: Another related indicator
- **Position Badge**: Badge showing the player's main position
- **Link**: "Player details" to view complete information

### Features

- **Expand all**: Link in the top right corner to expand all player cards
- **Player details**: Click on any card to see detailed player information

---

## üë• Team Performance (TOP 3)

This section shows the three teams with the best performance in the system.

### Information Displayed

For each team, you'll see:

- **Avatar**: Colored circle with team number or initial
- **Team Name**: Team's full name
- **Category**: Team category and level (e.g., Adult, Gold / U-17, Silver)
- **Number of Players**: Number of players registered in the team
- **Number of Trainings**: Number of trainings conducted
- **Average Attendance**: Average attendance percentage of team players
- **Link**: "View Statistics" to view detailed team analyses

### Features

- **View Statistics**: Click on any card to access complete team statistics
- **Sorting**: Teams are sorted by performance (average attendance)

---

## üìà Technical Training View

This section presents visual analyses of conducted trainings.

### Training Focus

Horizontal bar chart showing the distribution of fundamentals worked on in trainings:

- **Attack**: Number of trainings focused on attack
- **Reception**: Number of trainings focused on reception
- **Serve**: Number of trainings focused on serve
- **Setting**: Number of trainings focused on setting
- **Defense**: Number of trainings focused on defense
- **Block**: Number of trainings focused on block

Each bar visually shows the number of trainings for each fundamental, making it easy to identify the most worked-on areas.

### Trainings per Month

Vertical bar chart showing the distribution of trainings throughout the months:

- Monthly visualization of the number of trainings conducted
- Facilitates tracking of training frequency
- Allows identification of periods of higher or lower activity

---

## üìä Attendance Analysis

This section provides a detailed view of attendance and absences in trainings.

### General Attendance Rate

Main card displaying:

- **General Percentage**: Large highlight for the general attendance rate (e.g., 78%)
- **Subtitle**: "Group average"
- **Trend**: Comparison with the previous month (e.g., "‚Üë+6% compared to last month")
- **Statistical Summary**:
  - **Total Confirmations**: Total number of confirmations received
  - **Presences**: Total number of presences recorded
  - **Absences**: Total number of absences recorded

### Attendance by Team

List showing each team's attendance:

- **Team Name**: Team name
- **Attendance Percentage**: Team's attendance rate
- **Trend Indicator**: 
  - **‚Üë**: Increase compared to the previous month
  - **‚Üí**: No change compared to the previous month
  - **‚Üì**: Decrease compared to the previous month

---

## üèÜ Attendance Ranking

This section displays a detailed attendance ranking by team, showing each team's players sorted by performance.

### Information Displayed

For each team in the ranking, you'll see:

- **Team Avatar**: Colored circle with team identifier
- **Team Name**: Team's full name
- **Category**: Team category and level
- **Team Attendance Percentage**: Team's overall attendance rate
- **Player List**: Players sorted by attendance:
  - **Ranking Position**: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.
  - **Player Name**: Full or abbreviated name
  - **Position**: Player's position on the team
  - **Attendance Percentage**: Individual attendance rate
  - **Statistics**: Format "X/Y" (presences/total trainings)

### Features

- **Sorting**: Players sorted from highest to lowest attendance percentage
- **View by Team**: Each team is displayed in a separate card
- **Comparison**: Facilitates comparison of performance between teams and players
